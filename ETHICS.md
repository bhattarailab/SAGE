# Ethical Use Clause

This dataset is released for research purposes under the CC BY-SA 4.0 license. In addition to the terms of the license, users must comply with the following ethical use conditions.

## 1. No Re-identification
Users are strictly prohibited from attempting to re-identify any individuals represented in this dataset. This includes any effort to link samples, metadata, or derived features back to real-world identities.

## 2. Data Privacy and Security
Users must handle the dataset responsibly and implement appropriate safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or distribution.

Any suspected data breach, leakage, or unauthorized use involving this dataset must be reported promptly to the dataset maintainers.

## 3. Ethical and Regulatory Compliance
Users must ensure that their use of the dataset complies with all applicable ethical, legal, and institutional requirements, including (where applicable):
- Institutional Review Board (IRB) or ethics committee approvals
- Human subject research guidelines
- Data protection and privacy regulations

## 4. No Harmful or Misuse Applications
The dataset must not be used for any harmful purposes, including but not limited to:
- Surveillance or tracking of individuals
- Profiling or discrimination
- Deployment in clinical or diagnostic systems without proper validation and regulatory approval
- Any non-research or malicious applications

## 5. Responsible AI Commitment
Users are expected to follow Responsible AI principles, ensuring transparency, fairness, and safety in any models or systems developed using this dataset.

---

This ethical use clause aligns with principles from Responsible AI Licenses (RAIL) frameworks for safe and responsible dataset usage.
